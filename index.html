About me

I'm a bit of a modern day nomad ‐ enjoy travelling and not afraid to move onto new things taking on new challenges, going above and beyond from what it is to be expected. I have lived in 4 different countries thus far [Dominican Republic, USA, Australia and Korea] and speak 3 different languages fluently, although my brain tends to mix them up from time to time. 😅

Very hands‐on and love animals and nature! Most of my work is inspired by one or the other ~ or both! I've had a creative streak my entire life and I've slowly moved onto incorporating it onto the technological world. I like to make things from scratch (like this website!), kinda gives me a better sense of accomplishment, making me feel warm and fuzzy inside. 🤗
Currently focused on Front-end Development with HTML, CSS and Javascript, but have plans to move onto programming languages such as Python and Java.

I have a Bachelors in Animal and Veterinary Bioscience from the University of Sydney and currently work as an Animal Health Technologist, but have worked in several different industries.
